Built for the way news actually works

Claim-by-claim verification

Extracts 5–8 checkable claims and verifies each independently with statistics, quotes, attributions, stated events. Not a summary. Not a sentiment score.

Political lean labeling

Every source labeled Left to Right so you see where support comes from. Not just how many outlets agree, but whether they span the political spectrum.

Coverage divergence

CNN says X, Fox says Y, primary source says Z, all surfaced automatically. Identifies where outlets with different lean labels specifically disagree, and why it matters.

Never issues a true or false verdict.
Confidence levels and cited sources — you decide.
